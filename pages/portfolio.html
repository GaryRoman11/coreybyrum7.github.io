Accessibility
In my current role, accessibility is paramount. We welcome all types of users and strive to enable people with disabilities to perceive, understand, navigate, interact with, and contribute to our platform. As the sole UX/UI Engineer, accessibility is a primary focus.
In the above video, the website is accessible for users how do not have the ability to use a mouse. Traversal of the website is available via the arrow and tab keys. Also for users who may not have visibility, content is read aloud via a screen reader using the appropriate attributes.
